# Jun Goto - Personal Website

Personal academic website of Jun Goto, Associate Professor at the National Graduate Institute for Policy Studies (GRIPS), Tokyo.

🌐 **URL:** https://jungoto01.github.io/

## About

I am a development and political economist combining text-as-data and other unstructured sources with machine learning and causal inference to study how institutions, information, and social norms shape behavior in low- and middle-income countries.

## Structure

- `index.html` - Home (About)
- `research.html` - Research publications and working papers
- `teaching.html` - Teaching activities
- `cv.html` - Curriculum Vitae
- `assets/` - CSS, images, and other static files
- `docs/` - Paper PDFs and supplementary materials
